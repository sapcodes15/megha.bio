About Me

A Teacher Who Truly Cares

With more than 28 years of dedicated teaching, Mrs. Megha Anil Patil has shaped the minds of hundreds of students across all grade levels. Her classroom is where curiosity meets knowledge — where the magic of biology unfolds lesson by lesson.

From botany to genetics, from human physiology to environmental science, Mrs. Patil's teaching style blends conceptual clarity with real-world examples that students remember for life.

Teaches Biology across all grade levels — primary to higher secondary

Creates accessible video content on YouTube for students at home

Available for personalized private coaching sessions

Known for patience, encouragement, and making complex topics simple
